Characters

Across all the levels you will see and interact with a wide array of characters

Key Characters

Madeline

The main character of the game, Madeline is a red headed girl who suffers from mental health. She takes on the the challenge to climb Celeste Mountain to prove to herself that she can do hard things. She is sassy and lacks self confidence but she has the inexplicable ability to dash through the air and change the color of her hair.

Badeline

Renamed to Badeline by the Celeste Community, Part of You, is the personification of Madelines struggles and self-doubts. Often appearing to cause havoc in Madelines life she is there with the motivation to try and protect Madeline from getting more hurt.

Granny

Granny is the first character Madeline meets on your climb up the mountain. She is a little old lady that lives at the entrance of the mountain trail. She ominously warns Madeline that it isn't just any old hike but a hike filled with dangers and perils that Madeline might not be ready to see. She also appears multiple times on the mountain to give Madeline little pep talks. Madeline starts to grow fond of Granny and when Granny passes at the end of the game, Madeline takes it upon herself to say goodbye to Granny.

Bird

The bird is a blue crow creature that appears in the game to teach the player a new tech or to signal that Granny is near. In chapter 9, the bird plays a significant part of the plot as Madeline believes capturing the bird will bring back Granny.

Appears in

  • Chapter 9

Mr Oshiro

Mr Oshiro is the Manager at the long since abandoned Celestial Resort. He just wants to have one last guest stay within the walls of his exquisite hotel. Madeline sympathizes with Mr Oshiro and takes it upon herself to clean the hotel for him, despite him not appreciating the work himself.

Appears in

  • Chapter 3
