Introductions

Welcome to the Celeste Mountain Basecamp. Here we will get you trained up so you too can reach the summit The frozen mountain sleeps ahead but what might seem like a gentle giant ready to climb is actually the home to an adventure of self discovery.

The Game

Celeste is a 2d platformer. The player controls red haired Madeline. Madeline has the ability to jump through the air, climb up walls and dash through the air to reach new heights. The game was released in 2018 by Extremely OK Games on the following Consoles:
  • PC
  • Linux
  • Nintendo Switch
  • PS4
  • Xbox One
  • Google Stadia (Discontinued)

Real World Inspiration

Celeste is based on a mountian located on Vancouver Island, British Colombia.

Mount Celeste is the unofficial name for a mountain located on Vancouver Island, British Columbia. It shares the name Celeste with two peaks in the Cariboo region of the BC Interior. Within the boundaries of Strathcona Provincial Park, this peak lies at the north end of Rees Ridge. Iceberg Peak lies at the south end of this ridge.
